A breif history on the Batman.

  1. Bruce Wayne was born to parents Thomas and Martha Wayne in Gotham City.
  2. After witnessing the death of his parents, Bruce began on the path of vengeance against criminals.
    • His only caretaker to raise him was left up to their butler, Alfred Pennyworth, and Dr. Leslie Thompkins who arranged that Bruce would not be taken by Gotham's Social Services.
  3. For years Bruce spent his time studying criminology, forensics, and criminal psychology, and learned from manhunters and martial artists, mastering every fighting style. .
  4. Establishing a secret headquarters in the caves beneath his mansion, Bruce became Batman, a Dark Knight to protect Gotham and its citizens from vice and villainy. Alfred Pennyworth remained his confidant, tending to injuries and offering life advice.
